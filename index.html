Grower’s Note

This plant is the result of time spent listening — to the soil, to the light, to the silence between cycles. Each leaf, each crystalized edge, was shaped not by force but by patience.

I didn’t grow this just to harvest it. I grew it to understand it. To observe how something so delicate could also be so certain in its becoming. The process taught me that living well doesn’t mean rushing toward the end, but dwelling fully in each small stage of growth.

What you hold isn’t just flower — it’s a moment. And I hope it helps you stay in yours.
